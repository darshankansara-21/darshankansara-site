## What it does

A reusable Streamlit shell I drop new datasets into. Handles uploads, basic profiling, charting, and one-click sharing — without spinning up a heavy BI tool every time.

## Why it exists

Most one-off data questions don't need Power BI. They need a chart in 3 minutes. This is that.

## Stack

Streamlit · pandas · Plotly · containerised with Docker for easy deploy.
