## What it does

Type a half-formed thought, paste a link, or drop an article — get back a clean LinkedIn post with a hook, body, takeaway, and optional carousel slides.

## Why it exists

I kept losing good ideas because the gap between "thought" and "polished post" felt huge. This tool cuts that gap to about 30 seconds.

## Stack

Flask · OpenAI-compatible LLM API · simple SQLite store for drafts and posted history.
