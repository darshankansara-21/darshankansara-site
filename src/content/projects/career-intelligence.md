## What it does

Career Intelligence ingests your resume, the jobs you're applying to, and your background notes, then helps you:

- Rewrite resume bullets to actually match a specific job description
- Surface relevant experience you'd otherwise forget to mention
- Track applications and follow-ups
- Prep tailored interview answers grounded in your real history

## Why it exists

Generic AI resume tools spit out the same buzzword soup for everyone. I wanted something that *knew my actual career* and could reason about it like a thoughtful mentor.

## Stack

FastAPI backend · Next.js + Tailwind frontend · LLM-powered analysis · local vector store for resume + job history.
